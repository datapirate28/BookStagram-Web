Rich Dad, Poor Dad

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki is a groundbreaking personal finance book that challenges conventional wisdom about wealth and success. Published in 1997, the book tells the story of Kiyosaki's two fathers: his highly educated but financially struggling "poor dad" and his less educated but wealthy "rich dad." Through engaging anecdotes and practical lessons, Kiyosaki explores key concepts such as financial literacy, the importance of investing, and the difference between working for money and making money work for you. "Rich Dad Poor Dad" offers a unique perspective on achieving financial independence and building lasting wealth.
