12 Rules For Life

"12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos" is a thought-provoking self-help book written by Jordan B. Peterson, a renowned psychologist and professor. Published in 2018, the book offers twelve profound principles for navigating life's challenges and finding meaning in a complex world. Peterson draws on a wide range of disciplines, including psychology, philosophy, and religion, to provide practical advice and insights on topics such as personal responsibility, meaning, truth, and order. With its blend of intellectual rigor and accessible writing, "12 Rules for Life" serves as a guide for personal growth and a roadmap to a more fulfilling existence.
